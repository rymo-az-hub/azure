# Policy Exception Process

## When exception is allowed
- 業務要件上、どうしても回避できない場合のみ
- 代替案が検討済みであること

## Required fields
- Requested by / Owner
- Scope (MG/Subscription/RG/Resource)
- Policy name
- Reason & business justification
- Risk assessment
- Mitigation plan
- Expiration date (default 30 days)
- Approval (Platform owner)

## Review
- Weekly/Monthly: 期限前棚卸し
- Expired: 原則失効（必要なら再申請）
